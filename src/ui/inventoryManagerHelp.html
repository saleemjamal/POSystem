‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I don't have the PO number?

Check with the store manager or look at the delivery paperwork. Every delivery should have a PO number.

Q: What if the amount doesn't match the PO?

Record the actual amount received. The system tracks differences automatically - this helps us monitor distributor performance.

Q: Can I edit a GRN after creating it?

Contact system administrators (saleem@ or karima@) for corrections. It's better to be accurate the first time.

Q: What if goods arrive damaged?

Record the GRN for the amount actually usable, and add detailed notes about the damage for follow-up.

Q: How long do I have to record a GRN?

Record as soon as possible after delivery, but there's no strict deadline. Even late GRNs are tracked.

Q: What if no goods arrive for a PO?

Don't create a GRN. The system will automatically close the PO after 10 days and mark it as "No Receipt."
