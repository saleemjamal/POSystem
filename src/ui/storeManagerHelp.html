Step 2: Fill Order Information

Step 3: Enter Customer Details

  • Customer Name: Full name (required)

Step 5: Add Notes (Optional)

Include any special instructions, urgency, or customer preferences

Step 6: Submit

Click "Create Customer Order" - you'll get a CO number for tracking

üìäOrder Status Tracking

  • Pending: Order created, waiting for admin approval
  • Approved: Admin approved, converting to purchase order
  • Sent: Purchase order sent to distributor
  • Received: Goods received at store
  • Delivered: Customer has collected/received the order

üí°Best Practices

  • Get complete customer info: Name and at least one contact method
  • Be specific with new items: Include brand, model, or detailed description
  • Note urgency: Use the notes field if customer needs it by a specific date
  • Follow up: Check order status to keep customers informed

‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Q: What if the item isn't in our catalog?

Select "New Item" and provide a detailed description. Admin will review and source it.

Q: How long does approval take?

Typically within 24 hours during business days. Urgent orders can be flagged in notes.

Q: Can I modify an order after creating it?

Contact administrators for changes. It's better to get details right the first time.

Q: How do I know when goods arrive?

Check the CustomerOrders sheet or contact administrators for status updates.
